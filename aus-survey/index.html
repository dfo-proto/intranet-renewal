Accessible Usability Scale (AUS) Survey

The Accessible Usability Scale (AUS) takes less than ten minutes to complete and calculates a score between 0 and 100.

Please select one option from each of the following 10 questions.

1. I would use this web-based product frequently, if I had a reason to.
2. I found the web-based product unnecessarily complex.
3. I thought the web-based product was easy to use.
4. I think that I would need the support of another person to use all the features of this web-based product.
5. I found the various functions of the web-based product made sense and were compatible with my technology.
6. I thought there was too much inconsistency in how this web-based product worked.
7. I would imagine that most people with my assistive technology would learn to use this web-based product quickly.
8. I found the web-based product very cumbersome or awkward to use.
9. I felt very confident using the web-based product.
10. I needed to familiarize myself with the web-based product before I could use it effectively.
